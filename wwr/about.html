White Water Rafting Company

At White Water Rafting Company, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water.

History

Founded in 1995 by a group of adventure enthusiasts, our company started with just two rafts and a dream. We wanted to create a way for people to escape the city.

Over the last 30 years, we have grown into the region's premier rafting guide service, helping thousands of families and thrill-seekers discover the beauty of the river.
